Demonstration off neural document ranking

The video below shows a live search engine, based on Anserini, for two standalone neural ranking models that use sparse latent representations. The implementation can be found on the github repository accompanying this page. The Sparse Model is based on [Zamani et al., 2018] and the Random Zipfian Model uses random document and query representations with a Zipfian latent term frequency distribution. More information on why these two models are compared here can be found in my thesis (reference may be added later).
